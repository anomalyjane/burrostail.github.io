Welcome to BTF!

In 2016, we set up shop for the very first time at our local farmers' market, bringing our creative succulent arrangements to a warm reception in the metro region. Later, we took the Burro-mobile and headed to SoWa in Boston's South End to sling plants alongside some of the finest and funkiest vendors in town. Stay tuned for info on BTF's 2017 season! We can't wait to share our love of these amazing desert plants with you!

Market Picks

It's morning, but still dark. We take in the cool air while we load the Burro-Mobile with a fresh, happy batch of succulent arrangements for market. The pots range in size from itty bitties the size of an espresso cup all the way up to teapots, soup bowls, and antique flour sifters. We've searched high and low to find an interesting array of upcycled containers, carefully selected just the right combination of plants for each one, and tucked the plants snugly into their new digs. Now the time has come! We survey our crates full of green treasure... It's market day.

Custom Orders

Have a great container that you'd like to deck out with succulents? We're happy to create a custom arrangment that incorporate specific pots, plants, and other elements. We've created arrangements in just about every kind of container... from colanders to gnarly tree stumps. If it can hold dirt, it can hold succulents! Email us to find out more about custom orders.

Classes

Are you a group who's interested in taking a class? We are happy to lead groups in creative succulent arranging in the Boston and metrowest region, with the option for an all-materials-included class in the location of your choice. Creating your own succulent arrangement is good dirty fun. Please contact us to discuss your group's interests.
